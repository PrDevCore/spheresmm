# Data Deletion Instructions for SphereSMM Dashboard

## How to Delete Your Data

### Method 1: Email Request (Recommended)
Send an email to **prdevcore@email.com** with:
- **Subject:** `Data Deletion Request`
- **Body:** Your account email address and Firebase User ID (shown in the sidebar)

We will process your request within 7 days and send a confirmation email.

### Method 2: In-App (Partial Deletion)
1. Sign in to your account
2. Go to **Linked Channels** → click **Disconnect** on each connected account
3. Go to **Content Queue** → delete any scheduled or draft posts
4. Contact us at prdevcore@email.com to complete full account removal

## What Gets Deleted
- All stored social account references
- All post drafts, schedules, and published post records
- All analytics data and metrics
- Firebase Authentication account

## What Cannot Be Deleted
- Content already published to third-party platforms (X/Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn) — you must delete those directly on each platform
- Server logs (anonymized, no personal data)

## Confirmation
You will receive a confirmation email within 7 days of your request being processed.
